more pavlova

I don't know about you but after making that first pavlova, we were left wanting more. So we did all the searching for you and made a list of must try pavlova recipes. Here we have some more fun alternative toppings for your pavlova .Now that you have the basics you're free to play around with plenty of flavors. We have everything from light floral tones to full on tropical notes. Enjoy!


1. Rose Pistachio from the Fig and Pigs blog.
2. Strawberry Kiwi from the Skinny Taste website.
3. Mixed Berries from the Food Network website.
4. Rhubard Pistachio from the Martha Stewart website.
5. Passion Fruit Strawberry from the Martha Stewart website.
6. Toffee Crunch from Nigella's blog.
